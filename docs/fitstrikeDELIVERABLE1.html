01 / CONCEPT

Executive Summary

What is FitStrike?

FitStrike is an AI-powered, gamified fitness ecosystem that transforms mundane workouts into immersive multiplayer experiences. It combines:

  • GPS territory capture (like ZONR)
  • Gym workout tracking with RPG progression
  • Nutrition logging with AI suggestions
  • Community events & tournaments
  • Clan/Squad systems for social accountability
  • Live streaming & content creation tools

"Your body is the controller. Your city is the arena. Your progress is permanent."

07 / COMMUNITY

Community & Social Ecosystem

7.1 Clan System (Guilds)

Structure: 50 members max. Roles: Leader, Officer, Member, Recruit. Shared clan polygons and Clan Bank.

Progression: Levels 1-20 unlocking XP boosts, territory color bonuses, and special achievements.

7.2 Squad System (Parties)

Formation: 4-player parties with role selection: Scout Tank Support Striker.

Bonus: 1.5x shared territory multiplier for squadded operations.

7.3 Chat & Creators

Channels: Global, Local (5km), Clan, Squad, Direct, Event temporary.

Creator Program: Live workout streaming (720p/1080p), fan club tiers, and virtual gift economy (Coin splits).
